Giant Floating Baby Head (GFBH) Extension Options

Configure GFBH to function the way you want.

Image URLs:

 Want images other than the floating baby head? Enter the URLs of the images that should float across the web browser window.
  • Images with transparency are highly recommended.
  • Images will be displayed with their original dimensions.
  • Image URLs should begin with https://

Timing:

 When do you want the floating baby head to appear?
Random
Every minutes
At (24-hour clock)
You can trigger the appearance of the Baby Head by pressing Ctrl+Shift+F on most webpages.
The keyboard shortcut is configurable at the bottom of your Chrome extensions settings page: chrome://extensions
