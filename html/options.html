Giant Floating Baby Head (GFBH) Extension Options

Configure GFBH to function the way you want.

Image URLs: Want images other than the floating baby head? Enter the URLs of the images that should float across the web browser window.
  • Images with transparency are highly recommended.
  • Images will be displayed with their original dimensions.
  • Image URLs should begin with https://

Schedule: When do you want the floating baby head to appear?
Random
Every minutes
At (24-hour clock)
You can trigger the appearance of the GFBH by pressing Ctrl+Shift+F on most webpages.
The keyboard shortcut is configurable in your Chrome extensions settings: chrome://extensions/shortcuts

Duration: How long do you want it to take the floating baby head to cross the browser window?
Exactly ( seconds)
Random

Disabled Domains: Want to disable the floating baby head from appearing on certain websites? Enter the domains where the GFBH should not appear.
  • Subdomains will also be disabled (for example, 'google.com' would disable 'www.google.com' and 'drive.google.com').
  • The full domain will be matched (for example, 'drive.google.com' would not disable 'google.com' nor 'somedrive.google.com').
